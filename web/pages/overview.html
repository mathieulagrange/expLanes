Experimentation and testing are a fundamental part of scientific research. They're also extremely time-consuming, tedious, and error-prone unless properly managed. expLanes (EXPerimental PLANning for Engineering and Science) is here to take care for you of the management, running and reporting of your experiments, letting you focus on the creative part of the research work.

What can expLanes do for you?

  • Set up an experimental protocol once, and re-run it as often as needed with minimum hassle using new data, modified code, or alternative hypotheses
  • Automatically explore many combinations of operational parameters in a system.
  • Easily deploy your experiment on many computers on a network.
  • Re-use earlier results to only compute the later stages of a process, thus cutting down the running time.
  • Automatically produce neatly formatted tables and graphs to study the results and embed them in publications.
  • Keep experimental code, data and results organized in a standard way, to ease cooperation with others and allow you to go back painlessly on months-old or years-old work.
